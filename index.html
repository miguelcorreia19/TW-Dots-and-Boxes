DOTS AND BOXES

How To Play?

Players take turns joining two horizontally or vertically adjacent dots by a line.
A player that completes the fourth side of a square, a box, colors that box and must play again.
When all boxes have been colored, the game ends and the player who has colored more boxes wins. If the player completed the same number of boxes wins who spent less time.
